What Our Students Says

The annual college fee is comparatively affordable than many other colleges. It is around 30k annually. I qualified through CET and chose this college, so I feel that the total expenses for my education are affordable. I am a day scholar and until before lockdown, I traveled through a bus facility provided by the college, which cost me around 22k annually. There are various scholarships awarded by the college to people who are in dire need of it.

Christine Berkley

Campus life in college good. There is lots of events and sports activity keep happing every year. Yes teachers and student of college are supportive and friendly.
